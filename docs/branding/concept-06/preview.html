Veritra — Chain Link V identity

Concept 06 developed into a mark family. Two interlocked links draw the V: an end-to-end connection between exactly two parties, with the receiver's link passing over and the sender's tucking under it, its tip visible through the loop. Indigo is the sender, sky is the receiver.

Primary mark

Gradient two-link mark with a keyline tuck-under. Works on light, dark, and photographic backgrounds.

Monochrome

Single-ink variant for print, engraving, and one-color contexts. The keyline gap carries the interlock.

White knockout

For dark and brand-color surfaces.

App icon

Mark at 82% on an ink squircle. Fits iOS and Android adaptive-icon safe zones.

Favicon / small sizes

Simplified variant: heavier strokes, no keyline gap — it vanishes below ~24 px.

Palette

Indigo (sender) and sky (receiver) endpoints, ink for surfaces.
